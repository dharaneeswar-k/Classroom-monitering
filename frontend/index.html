How Smart Classroom AI Works

From camera to report in seconds — fully automated, no manual intervention.

01

Camera Captures the Classroom

When a faculty starts a session, the AI layer activates the live camera feed and begins scanning the classroom in real time.

02

AI Identifies & Tracks Students

Our face recognition engine matches detected faces against enrolled students and continuously monitors engagement scores throughout the session.

03

Reports Generated Automatically

When the session ends, attendance records are instantly saved per student. Faculty and admins can review dashboards and export reports at any time.

Features

Everything You Need, Nothing You Don't

A complete suite of tools built for modern academic institutions.

Real-Time Face Recognition

Powered by DeepFace, the system identifies enrolled students from a live camera stream with high accuracy — even in varying lighting conditions.

Engagement Score Tracking

Beyond presence, the AI measures student engagement across each session, flagging behavior signals like distraction or drowsiness in real time.

Automated Attendance Records

No more manual registers. Attendance is written to the database the moment a session ends, with full session-level detail preserved.

On-Duty (OD) Management

Students can submit OD requests for events. Faculty review and approve/reject them from their dashboard, and approved ODs guard attendance automatically.

Role-Based Access Control

Three separate dashboards — Student, Faculty, Admin — each tailored to their workflows with secure JWT-based authentication.

Live Dashboard & Analytics

Faculty see a real-time attendance feed, AI video stream, and classroom metrics live. Admins get system-wide oversight of all classrooms and users.
